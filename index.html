Whale Fall

You are a marine biologist stationed on the bottom of the ocean floor to monitor the micro-ecosystem of a dead whale - a whale fall.The research lab has created a new mini-pod - with just enough room for one person to go down in. As you observe the strange and wondrous world of bizarre, thriving sea life, you start to believe the shadows out of the corner of your eyes are more than just your mounting anxiety at being trapped down here.
