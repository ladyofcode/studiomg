Whalefall

You are a marine biologist stationed on the bottom of the ocean floor to monitor the micro-ecosystem of a dead whale, referred to as a Whale fall. Usually, this is done by a remote camera, but the research lab has created a new mini-pod - with just enough room for one person to go down in. As you observe the strange and wondrous world of bizarre sea life that thrives in this micro-ecosystem, you start to believe the shadows out of the corner of your eyes are more than just your mounting anxiety at being trapped down here.
